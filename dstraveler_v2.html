FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our services.

Still have questions?
What services does Destiny Travel Consultants provide?

We provide comprehensive visa and travel consultation services including Student Visas, Visitors Visas, Work Visas, Conference & Business Visas, Embassy Form Completion, Visa Interview Preparation, and personalised Travel Consulting. We serve clients applying to destinations worldwide including the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, and many more countries.

How long does the application process take?

Processing times vary by destination country, visa type, and individual circumstances. During your initial free consultation, we will give you a realistic timeline based on your specific case. We always begin your application as early as possible to avoid any delays.

What happens if my application is unsuccessful?

In the unlikely event of an unsuccessful application, we thoroughly analyse the refusal reason and advise you on the best course of action — whether that's reapplying with stronger documentation, appealing the decision, or exploring alternative visa pathways. We stand by our clients through every step.

How much does your service cost?

Our service fees depend on the type of visa and the complexity of your case. We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden costs. During your free initial consultation, we will provide a full breakdown of what is included and the associated fee before you commit to anything.

Does Destiny Travel Consultants help with visa interviews?

Absolutely. Visa Interview Preparation is one of our core services. We conduct thorough mock interview sessions, coach you on how to answer questions confidently, advise on appropriate dress and etiquette, and ensure you walk into your embassy interview fully prepared.
